# Feedback Agent
  Put relevant feeback into categories to identify which areas they are for and who needs to deal with it

## What this automation does
  This automation categories feedback and identifies relevant areas by:
  - Detect new customer feedback on sheets or CRM
  - Process customer information
  - AI Agent cateforizes customer feedback and identifies area
  - Emails get sent out to team based on "categories" and "areas":
    - Categories: - "question", "problem", "suggestion", or "other"

## Who this is for
  - Plumbers
  - Real estate agents
  - service based businesses 
  - Any business that values customer feedback

## Key features
  - CRM Integration
  - AI powered agent
  - Customer feedback get grouped
  - works 24/7 with no manual input
  - Instant emails get sent to team

## How it works
  1) Google Sheet or CRM gets updated on new bookings
  2) Customer feedback and info is gathered into workflow
  3) AI Agent cateforizes and identifies feedback area
  5) Emails get sent out team regarding feedback based on category and area

## Tech Stack
  - n8n (workflow automation)
  - Google Gemini (Chat Model)
  - Gmail (communication channel)
  - Google sheets (lead storage)
